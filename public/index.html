Work Locations

Jhajjar Sector-6

Sector 6 is located in the heart of Jhajjar City. It is located between two roads connecting major cities of Gurgaon & Rewari. It is the first sector allotted in Jhajjar 2008. Since then it's growth has been exponential.It has all amenities including 24*7 electricity , Gated main Roads, 4 Parks , Proper Water Facility & sewage Facilities .

Distance from major Prime Locations:

  • Nearby Education Institutes : XLRI MBA , Sehwag International School , R.E.D School
  • IGT Airport - 1 hour
  • AIMS Jhajjar - 15 Min
  • Reliance Industries - 10 Min

Jhajjar Sector-9

Sector 9 is the 2nd Sector Launched by Haryana Urban Authority Development in Jhajjar City. It is located near the education HUB of City. Nehru College, ITI all are located 5 min away from the location. This Sector is still in the developing phase but the amenities are available including water , sewerage , electricity , rain water discharge etc.

Distance from major Prime Locations:

  • PGIMS Rohtak - 40 Min
  • GITM College - 20 Min
  • AIMS Jhajjar - 10 Min
  • Police Line - 15 Min
  • Bahadurgarh City - 25 min
